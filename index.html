Her profiles information

Hezhiniang is the Cirnosoft Corporation’s mascot, First appeared in “Put Feet on Table S5E04” She is a tengu, works at the Cirnosoft Corporation. she is also a reporter for CSV News. Although she works as a reporter like other tengu, she is not very intelligent and sometimes says something rather ignorant.In fact, she's about the same age as the two vampires at the Scarlet Devil Mansion (actually a little younger than them), and she's very weak compared to the other tengu.

She lives in the Tengu Gathering Place in Yokai Mountain, to the western part of the Tengu Gathering Place

Hezhiniang has dark blue hair and is waist-length. Wearing a purple tokin hat. Blue eyes, hair pinned with the Cirnosoft logo made of cloth or plexiglass. The color scheme of spring clothes and autumn clothes is basically the same, while the colors of summer 2 clothes are mainly light colors. Single-tooth tall clogs are generally not worn (because they are unsteady on her feet), and in winter they are changed to boots. She usually holds a personal digital assistant or tablet pc.
